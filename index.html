Bridging the divide, one state at a time

Ruby Red States Blues is a space for thoughtful conversation, local stories, and practical ideas from communities that shape national debates every day.

About

Our mission

We highlight perspectives from across the political spectrum in predominantly conservative regions, focusing on shared challenges, local solutions, and respectful debate.

Our approach

Through interviews, essays, and community spotlights, we amplify voices that are often reduced to stereotypes in national conversations.

Key topics

Policy & governance

  • Local leadership: How city and county decisions shape daily life.
  • Representation: What residents want from their elected officials.
  • Accountability: Tracking promises, budgets, and outcomes.

Community & culture

  • Work & wages: Stories from small towns and growing metros.
  • Education: Classrooms, campuses, and career paths.
  • Civic life: Faith groups, nonprofits, and local organizers.
